## Email (himalaya)

Use `himalaya` for email operations:

- `himalaya list` — list recent emails
- `himalaya list -f INBOX` — list inbox
- `himalaya read <id>` — read a specific email
- `himalaya send --to user@example.com --subject "Subject" --body "Body"` — send email
- `himalaya reply <id> --body "Reply text"` — reply to an email
- `himalaya forward <id> --to user@example.com` — forward
- `himalaya delete <id>` — delete
